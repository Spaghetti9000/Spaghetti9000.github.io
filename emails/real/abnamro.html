Tips from our expert Kathelijne
 

The ABN AMRO app lets you bank anywhere you like. Our fraud expert Kathelijne gives a few tips about banking extra securely on your phone.

  • If you’re worried about someone looking over your shoulder when you log in, secure the app with facial recognition or your fingerprint. That way, nobody can see your personal codes.
  • Never do your banking on a public Wi-Fi network, as these networks aren’t adequately secured.
  • Don’t forget to log out when you’ve finished banking.
